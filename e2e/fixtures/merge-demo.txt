The Ashen Vow

Aldric was a weary knight who had failed to protect his home village of Eldermoor
from the raiders. Haunted by the smell of smoke and the screams he could not stop,
he wandered the northern roads seeking a redemption he was not sure he deserved.
Aldric carried a notched longsword and wore his late captain's dented pauldron.

His only companion was Mira, a sharp-tongued cartographer who had lost her sister
in the same raid. Mira did not trust Aldric at first, but their shared grief slowly
became a fragile alliance.

The village of Eldermoor sat at the edge of the Whispering Fen, a marshland said to
swallow those who wandered it after dark. It was here that Aldric's story began, and
here that he swore his ashen vow: never again would he stand by while the helpless burned.
