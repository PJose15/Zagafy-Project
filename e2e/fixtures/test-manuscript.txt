Chapter 1: The Beginning

It was a dark and stormy night when Elena first discovered the letter hidden in the attic. The old house creaked under the weight of decades of secrets, and the rain hammered against the windows like a thousand tiny fists demanding entry.

She held the yellowed envelope up to the dim light of the single bare bulb, her fingers trembling. The handwriting was unmistakable — it belonged to her grandmother, who had been dead for fifteen years.

"Dear Elena," it began. "If you are reading this, then the time has come."

Chapter 2: The Discovery

Marco found her sitting on the attic floor at dawn, surrounded by scattered pages. His concern was immediate and genuine — that was the trouble with Marco. He always noticed too much.

"What happened?" he asked, kneeling beside her.

Elena slid the letter beneath her knee. "Nothing. Just looking through old things."

The lie came easily. Too easily.
